Professional Training Programs

Park HIIT Circuit (20 Minutes)

  1. Jump Squats: Explosive movement for 30 seconds to build power.
  2. Push-Ups: Maintain a strong plank position for 30 seconds.
  3. Bench Step-Ups: 30 seconds each leg using a park bench for height.
  4. Mountain Climbers: High-intensity core work for 30 seconds.
  5. Rest & Recovery: 60 seconds rest between rounds. Repeat 4 total times.

Home Full-Body Strength (45 Minutes)

  1. Goblet Squats: 3 sets of 12. Focus on depth and keeping the chest up.
  2. Push-Up Variations: 3 sets of 10-15. Adjust hand width to challenge yourself.
  3. Resistance Band Rows: 3 sets of 15. Squeeze the shoulder blades together.
  4. Bulgarian Split Squats: 3 sets of 10 each leg. Elevate the rear foot for intensity.
  5. Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 12. Use a sturdy chair or coffee table at home.
  6. Core Plank: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds. Keep your body in a straight line.

Outdoor Gym Circuit (30 Minutes)

  1. Pull-Ups: 3 sets of 5-10. Use assisted machines if needed for form.
  2. Parallel Bar Dips: 3 sets of 8-12. Target the triceps and lower chest.
  3. Ab Bench Leg Raises: 3 sets of 15. Control the tempo on the way down.
  4. Steady Run: 5 minutes at a moderate pace to flush the muscles.
  5. Repeat: Complete the entire sequence 2 times for a 30-minute session.

Professional Tips for Success

  1. Progressive Overload: Challenge yourself by increasing reps or reducing rest.
  2. Consistency Over Intensity: Regular movement beats occasional burnout sessions.
  3. Hydration & Sun: Vital for outdoor training in East London's summer months.
  4. Warm-Up/Cool Down: Spend 5 minutes on dynamic stretching to prevent injury.
  5. Recovery: Prioritize 7-9 hours of sleep to allow muscle repair.

Remember: the best workout is the one you do consistently. Mix and match these routines based on your goals. Whether you're in your living room or a local park, progress is always within reach!
