About Your Personal Trainer: Dean Burt

Certified Personal Trainer Since 2019

I'm Dean, a certified personal trainer since 2019 with a lifelong passion for fitness. My mission is to help you achieve your health and fitness goals through personalised training that enhances technique, builds strength, and prevents injury.

Building Strength Beyond the Physical

Fitness is more than physical—it's about cultivating a strong mindset. Your body can achieve more than you think, and I'm here to help you overcome mental and physical barriers. Recognising that everyone is unique, I tailor each session to your individual goals and challenges, ensuring optimal results while minimising injury risk.

Customized Workout Plans for Your Goals

We all have reasons for starting a fitness journey. Maybe you're just beginning or need a little help along the way—we’re here to help. Do you want to achieve that summer body? Are you preparing for a special occasion like a holiday or wedding? Dream of completing a marathon? Whatever your goal, we have tailored workout plans to suit your needs.
