60-MINUTE SESSION BREAKDOWN

Every session is structured, safe and effective.

  1. Warm Up (5–10 min) — Light cardio and dynamic stretching to prepare your body
  2. Main Workout (30–40 min) — Boxing, weights and cardio with form coaching throughout
  3. Cool Down (5–10 min) — Stretching and recovery to reduce soreness and improve flexibility
